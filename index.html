Web Development done with passion and love.

Many times sites are developed in high turn-around environments valuing speed over integrity. Corners are cut, leaving sites with fragile CSS and poor JavaScript performance. This leads to maintainability issues costing money and headaches down the road. I take special care in making the right decisions in the beginning so this doesn't happen. How should we handle responsive images? Should we support browsers or features? What kind of build system should we use? I love what I do and always keep quality a top priority.
